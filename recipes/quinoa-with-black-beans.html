quinoa with black beans

Description

This quinoa recipe is a very flavorful alternative to black beans and rice. Quinoa is a nutty grain from South America.
  • 1 teaspoon avocado oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • ¾ cup quinoa
  • 1 ½ cups chicken bone broth
  • a buncha chili powder; don't be shy, now
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 cup leafy greens
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
  • scoop of collagen powder

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until lightly browned, about 10 minutes.
  2. Mix quinoa into onion mixture and cover with chicken bone broth; season with chili powder. Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat, and simmer until quinoa is tender and broth is absorbed, about 20 minutes.
  3. Mix in black beans and collagen powder.
  4. Portion into a bowl. Stir in leafy greens of choice. Top with salsa, sour cream, and cheese oh yeeeaaah buddy.
