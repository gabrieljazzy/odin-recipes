2 ingredient healthy bagels

these bagels taste of yogurt and grain. that's cuz that's what they are lol. great with greens, a soft-boiled egg, and herb butter.

ingredients

  • oat flour (i just grind up steel cut oats in a blender, or you can use any flour you like)
  • greek yogurt
  • egg wash (optional but HIGHLY recommended)
  • everything bagel seasoning (optional but HIGHLY recommended)
  • ground flax meal (optional)

directions

  1. mix together flour and yogurt in a bowl, then spread onto and knead on lightly floured parchment paper
  2. form dough into donut shapes
  3. if using egg wash, brush on, and if using everything bagel seasoning, sprinkle over egg wash along with flax meal if using
  4. bake at 350 for 20 min or until golden brown
